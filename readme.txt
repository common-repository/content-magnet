== Frequently Asked Questions ==

Start with the main CM tab and input your details from whatever advertising units you will be using.

AFter adding your advertiser information you have two options.  Either go to "Gather Trends" or "Setup Posts."  Use "Gather Trends" first if you do not have a preference for subject matter.  Add a few trends and then go to "Setup Posts."  Fill in each post details and let them run.  It is very straight forward.  If you have any questions, feel free to email me at terry@revolutionvps.net.
